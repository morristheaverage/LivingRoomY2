Instructions

  • Press/hold arrow keys to twist left and right and move forwards and backwards
  • Use q and e buttons to tilt the camera view
  • Press/hold space bar to animate chairs
  • Use a and d buttons to tilt the fish tank
  • If textures do not display make sure you are hosting the website directory in a server e.g. npm http-server
  • Press L to toggle the light on/off and also press r, g and b buttons to individually toggle red, blue and green componenets of the light colour
